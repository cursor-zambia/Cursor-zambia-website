ABOUT THE COMMUNITY

Where code
meets craft

Cursor Zambia is a collective of developers, designers, and technologists united by a shared belief: that AI is not replacing developers, it's elevating them. Based in Lusaka, we are building Zambia's most vibrant community around the Cursor IDE and AI-assisted development.

Through workshops, hack nights, mentorship, and open-source collaboration, we create spaces where curiosity meets capability. Whether you're writing your first line of code or architecting complex systems, there's a place for you here.
